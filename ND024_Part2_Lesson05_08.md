# Lesson 5.8 Fonts

For reading, the amount of words per line shouldn't be too little or too much. 

Ideal measure, length of a line, ranges from 45-90 characters per line. We should aim for **65 cpl.**

- 16px base font is suggested. 
- 1.2em line height.
- 14px is too small.
