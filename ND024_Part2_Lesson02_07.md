# Lesson 2.7 Quiz: How wide is the viewport?

##### Quiz and solution:
Phone
640px wide, DPR = 2
**DIPs = 320px**

Phablet
768px wide, DPR = 2.5
**DIPs = 307.2px**

Tablet
1024px wide, DPR = 1
**DIPs = 1024px**

Laptop browser
800px wide, DPR = 1
**DIPs = 800px**
